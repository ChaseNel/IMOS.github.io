Help > Vehicle Instructions > Assign Vehicle > Steps
Steps

Step 1: Select the Assign Vehicle Button on the Vehicle Card on the home page.

Step 2: Select the Assign button in the vehicle detail table on the vehicle detail screen.

Step 3: Click on the OK button on the confirmation notification to confirm the assignment of the vehicle or the cancel button to return to the Vehicle detail screen.
